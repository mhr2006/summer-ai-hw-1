Making the account.
Deciding if you want to create your new account.
Or managing your account.
Or if you want to send/recieve messages.
Or quit the app.
If you want to create new account you will be told to put your:
Name, Date of birth, set a password, confirm the password, make a username.
Then your account will be made.
IF else you click manage your account, you will be asked to chose:
If you want to view your friends (click)
If you want to add friends (click):
Then add their username and add them.
If you want to block your friends that tap their username:- (click block)
If you want to send/recieve messages: (click username)
check messages or type a message and click send.
If you are done then press quit app.
